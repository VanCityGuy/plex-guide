Welcome to Plex!

What is Plex?

Plex is like a streaming service but hosted by your friend. It lets you watch movies, TV shows, and more from your friend's personal media library on any device. It's a great way to enjoy content without the hassle of managing your own collection!

Where is Plex Available?

Plex is available globally and works on most devices, including smartphones, tablets, smart TVs, computers, and streaming devices like Roku, Fire TV, Apple TV, and Xbox. There's an app for each of these platforms, so you can access it virtually anywhere with an internet connection!

1. Getting Started! Go to Plex.tv

2. Click on Sign Up

3. Sign up using one of the methods provided

4. Once your account is created, Go to app.plex.tv and when prompted change privacy settings

    Go to both of the indicated sections
    Change everything to Private
    Change to Off

5. Click on the settings icon located in the top right

6. Within settings, click on Manage Library Access

7. You should see the server you have been invited to here. If necessary click the check mark to accept access

8. Next, click the home icon to return to the main page

9. From home, select 'More'

10. You will see the server with the following libraries: 4K Local, Audiobooks, Movies, TV Shows

11. Hover over each and click the three dots to Pin all four libraries (and playlists if you intend to use them)

12. For the Plex categories, Unpin the following: Discover, Live TV, Movies & Shows, Music, Watchlist

13. Click on Pinned, your menu should now look like the image below

Quality Settings:

14. Next, click on the Settings icon, then go to Quality in the sidebar under Plex Web

15. Under Internet Streaming, beside Video Quality choose Maximum

16. Uncheck "Use recommended settings" and set both Home Streaming and Internet Streaming to Maximum. Click Save Changes, then click the home icon in the top left corner to return to the main page

FAQ:

  • Mobile Playback Fee: There's a one-time fee of $5 for unlocking mobile playback. This is charged by Plex via your mobile app store, not by me.
  • Account Sharing: Please don't share your account. If you have friends interested in joining, let me know, and I'll send them an invitation.
  • Requests: Requests can be made here: https://request.bigbearplex.me. Just sign in via Plex when prompted.
  • Reporting Issues: If you encounter any issues with video files, audio, language issues, etc., please report them in Plex or Overseer so they can be addressed promptly.
  • Best Experience with Plex: For the best experience with Plex, follow the instructions provided for each platform or device. I personally recommend Chromecast or Nvidia Shield as the best devices.
  • Platform Improvements & Downtime: Sometimes I make updates and have to shut down the server. Usually, I try to do it when no one is watching, but if the server is down temporarily, go touch grass. I'll try to communicate major outages to users in advance.
  • Library Quality: I try to get all movies in 1080p and all TV in at least 720p if not 1080p. If something is really poor quality while at maximum streaming quality, please report an issue, and I'll replace the file.
